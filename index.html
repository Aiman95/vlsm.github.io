Variable-Length Subnet Mask(VLSM)Calculation Explained.


Introduction

What is VLSM?,Variable-Length Subnet Mask(VLSM) is an algorithm that allows for the creation of sub-networks on a large network and, therefore, increases the overall capacity of the net enabling system administrators/Networking Experts to subdivide a single network into more than one smaller subnet. This process is known as Subnetting . VLSM enables one to minimize the complexity of the of IP configurations of the network and provides efficient use of the entire address.
